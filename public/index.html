FAQ

Common questions from parents

We've heard them all.

Do you offer all sports every session?

Not all at once. Coach Mark runs sport-specific sessions and rotates based on the season and what families are interested in. Baseball is the featured sport this spring. New sports are added regularly — follow along or reach out to find out what is coming next.

My kid has never played before. Is that okay?

That's literally the whole point! Coach Mark specializes in first-timers. Zero experience needed — not even knowing what the sport is.

Can I book just one session and see how it goes?

Absolutely. Gamedays are available. No pressure to buy a pack, no commitment to anything after that first session.

Can we fill a session with our friend group?

Yes! Grab 4–6 families, all book the same time slot, and make it your own little crew. It's the most fun way to do it.

What ages are sessions for?

Ages 4 through 9. Too young for most leagues, but exactly the right age to start learning and building that love of sport.

Do we need to bring equipment?

Nope. Coach Mark supplies everything you need. Just bring your kid in comfortable clothes and sneakers. That said, if your child has their own glove, bat, or set of golf clubs they love, bring them! There is nothing better than a kid playing with gear that already feels like theirs.

What if my kid wants to try different sports?

Each session is sport-specific, but your pack sessions can be used across any sport. Great for kids who haven't found their thing yet.

Ready to book your kid's first session?

No commitment. Just a good time on the field, court, or diamond. Pick a slot and let's go.
